# **App Name**: Gaia Loop

## Core Features:

- Smart Waste Scanner: Use the device's camera to identify waste material and suggest the best disposal or reuse method. Utilizes an AI model to recognize different types of materials.
- Waste Footprint Tracker: Track user's waste footprint and suggest daily micro-habits to reduce waste. Provides data of location and its waste management practices.
- Community Reward System: Award EcoPoints for recycling, composting, and other eco-friendly actions. EcoPoints can be redeemed for local discounts or donations.
- Waste-to-Value Marketplace: Connect people or small businesses with waste materials to recyclers, composters, or artists for reuse. Facilitates turning waste into a valuable resource.
- AI Local Policy Advisor: Provide local governments or NGOs the tool to simulate the environmental impact of different policy scenarios, such as plastic bans or composting hubs, using AI-driven environmental modeling.
- Voice + Chat Companion (Gaia): An AI assistant that can answer questions about waste disposal, recycling, composting, policies, and sustainable living. Continuously learns from global data.
- Environmental Education Hub: Provide a space where people can learn about environmental topics such as recycling, waste management, decomposition, and biogas.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest green (#32CD32) to represent nature and sustainability.
- Background color: Light green (#E5F7E5) for a fresh, clean feel.
- Accent color: Earthy brown (#A0522D) for grounding and reliability.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif that combines a modern look with some warmth.
- Use simple, intuitive icons representing different types of waste, recycling processes, and eco-actions.
- Clean, user-friendly layout with clear information hierarchy to guide users through the app's features.
- Subtle animations for positive feedback and rewards, encouraging continued engagement.
